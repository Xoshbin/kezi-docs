# Tutorials

Tutorials are learning-oriented lessons that lead the user through a series of steps to complete a project. They are designed for beginners or users learning a new feature.

## Structure
- **Goal**: Learning
- **Tone**: Instructional, Teacher-like ("Let's do this")
- **Content**: A complete, successful workflow. Avoid edge cases or options.

## Examples
- "Creating your first Invoice"
- "Setting up your Company Profile"
